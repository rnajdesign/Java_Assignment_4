# How to Use

1. **Add a Task**
   - Click *Add Task*.
   - Fill in the details.
   - Click *Save*.

2. **Mark as Completed**
   - Check the box in the “Completed” column.

3. **Save Tasks**
   - Click the *Save* button to store tasks locally.

4. **Load Tasks**
   - On app start, saved tasks will be loaded automatically.

5. **Share Tasks**
   - Click *Share* to copy the current task list to your clipboard.
